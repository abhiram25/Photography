Behind the Lens

My name is Priyanka Reddy and I am originally from Chicago, IL. I got married in June 2014, and moved to Maryland with my loving husband. Although I live and breathe Chicago, Maryland is finally starting to grow on me and feels a lot like home.


Other than being passionate about cooking, photography has been my passion. Growing up, I was always the one who loved being in front of the camera and now I like to be behind the scenes helping friends and family create a lifetime of beautiful memories by capturing those special moments.
